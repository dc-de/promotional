Red Eclipse v1.0 (Ides Edition) Out Now

After a lot of hard work, the Red Eclipse Team is proud to present their first release, "Ides Edition" is now available as of Tuesday the 15th of March 2011.

Downloads:
* Windows: http://redeclipse.net/download/windows
* Linux: http://redeclipse.net/download/linux
* OS X: http://redeclipse.net/download/macos
* Other/All: http://redeclipse.net/download/combined

Among the things you can enjoy in Red Eclipse are:

* Deathmatch, Capture the Flag, Defend the Flag, Bomber Ball, with tons more options combining mutators.
* Lots of stuff to tweak. Create your own spin on gameplay or weapons using server side variables.
* Agile gameplay which includes impulse dashing, wall running/kicking, melee attacks, and a jetpack mutator.
* The builtin editor from Cube Engine 2 that allows you to cooperatively edit maps online in real time.

If you like the game, please consider helping us raise the AU$294 for the creation of our new player model, which we have freely licensed under CC-BY-SA. Donate at our website, http://www.redeclipse.net/donate

We hope you enjoy playing it as much as we've enjoyed creating it.
